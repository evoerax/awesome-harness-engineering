# Grill Me

Relentlessly interview the user about a plan until reaching shared understanding.

## Process

1. **Start with the plan** — user presents what they want to build
2. **Ask one question at a time** — walk down each branch of the decision tree
3. **Provide recommended answer** — don't just ask, give your opinion
4. **If answer is in codebase** — explore codebase instead of asking
5. **Resolve dependencies** — decisions that depend on each other, resolve one-by-one
6. **Continue until done** — every branch resolved, shared understanding reached

## Anti-Patterns

- Asking 5 questions at once
- Accepting vague answers
- Skipping questions because "it seems obvious"
- Not providing your recommended answer
